MTK USB Drivers

Bryan Heller July 2026

What these drivers do

MTK USB drivers help Windows recognize MediaTek-based phones when connecting them for flashing, servicing, or firmware work. They are important for stable communication between the phone and software tools.

Why they are needed

Without the correct driver, many flashing tools will fail to detect the phone or show an unstable connection. Installing the proper driver helps the device appear correctly in the software.

  • MediaTek device detection
  • Better flashing tool compatibility
  • Stable USB communication
  • Useful for service and repair work
